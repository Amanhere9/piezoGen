How Piezoelectric Technology Works

Our innovative piezoelectric generators harness the power of human movement, converting every step into clean, renewable electricity through advanced crystal technology.

The Science Behind Every Step

Piezoelectric crystals generate electrical charge when subjected to mechanical stress. Our patented floor tiles contain high-efficiency piezoelectric materials that capture the kinetic energy from footsteps and convert it into usable electricity.
